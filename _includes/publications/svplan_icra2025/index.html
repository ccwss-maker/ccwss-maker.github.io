Swept volume-aware trajectory planning and mpc tracking for multi-axle swerve-drive amrs

Accepted by IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) , May 2025

Tianxin Hu*, Shenghai Yuan*, Ruofei Bai, Xinhang Xu, Yuwen Liao, Fen Liu, Lihua Xie

We propose a swept volume-aware trajectory planning and MPC tracking framework for multi-axle AMRs, enabling real-time path optimization that minimizes swept volume and enhances maneuverability in confined environments.
