CleanUpBench: Embodied Sweeping and Grasping Benchmark

Under review for an international AI conference

Wenbo Li*, Guanting Chen*, Tao Zhao, Jiyao Wang, Tianxin Hu, Yuwen Liao, Weixiang Guo, Shenghai Yuan

We introduce CleanUpBench, a reproducible benchmark for evaluating embodied agents in realistic indoor cleaning tasks. Built on NVIDIA Isaac Sim, it enables systematic assessment of navigation, manipulation, and efficiency for dual-mode service robots combining sweeping and grasping.
